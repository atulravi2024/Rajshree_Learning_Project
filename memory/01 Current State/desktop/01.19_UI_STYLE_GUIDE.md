## 🎨 VIBRANT-SOFT PALETTE (HSL)
We use a specific HSL (Hue, Saturation, Lightness) range to ensure colors are engaging but not eye-straining for children.

| Category | Primary Color | HSL Target | Use Case |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| **Varnamala** | Pastal Purple | `280, 70%, 93%` | Alphabet Backgrounds |
| **Math** | Mint Green | `140, 60%, 93%` | Numbers & Tables |
| **Names** | Peach Orange | `25, 100%, 93%` | Animals, Fruits, etc. |
| **Action** | Soft Blue | `210, 80%, 91%` | Buttons & Overlays |

### Master Variables
- `--primary-pink`: `#fce4ec` (Welcome Screen)
- `--soft-blue`: `#bbdefb` (Glass Bubbles)
- `--accent-orange`: `#ffb74d` (Active Highlighting)

## ♿ ACCESSIBILITY (WCAG 2.1)
Although designed for children, we maintain high legibility standards.

- **Target Contrast**: `4.5:1` for body text.
- **Interactable Size**: All buttons are minimum `48px x 48px` to support undeveloped motor skills.
- **Visual Only Mode**: The app remains functional even if audio fails (High-contrast emojis).
