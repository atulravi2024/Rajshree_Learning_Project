## 🏗️ MENU ARCHITECTURE (Deep-Dive)
The "Khel-Kood" section is designed to transition the child from passive learning to active participation through interactive play.

### 🏢 CURRENT STATE: VOCABULARY MODE
- **Role**: Cultural awareness & vocabulary acquisition.
- **Access**: Step 2 (Main) ➔ Step 3 (Sub: Games) ➔ Step 4 (Learning Cards).
- **Existing Content**: 26 items (e.g., लूडो, शतरंज, पिठ्ठू, गिल्ली-डंडा, कंचे).
- **Behavior**: Standard 1x1 Card Grid; plays Hindi audio + 300ms fade-in animation.

## ⚙️ TECHNICAL SETTINGS & STATE
- **GameState Object**: Future `window.GameState` will track session scores and progress.
- **Reward Logic**: Virtual "Digital Stickers" 🎉 for every 3 correct answers or a completed "Level".
- **Audio Feedback**: 
    - Success: `success_cheer.mp3`
    - Hint/Try Again: `try_again_soft.mp3`
- **UI Constraint**: Defensive design disables "Slideshow" mode during Games to prevent auto-scrolling issues.
