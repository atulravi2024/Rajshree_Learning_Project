## 🏗️ ARCHITECTURAL TERMS

### Step [1-5] Overlay
The multi-layered category selection system.
- **Step 1**: Welcome/Intro.
- **Step 2**: Main Domain (Varnamala, Ganit, etc.).
- **Step 3**: Sub-Category (Swar, Numbers, etc.).
- **Step 4**: Detailed Set (1-10, 1-100, etc.).
- **Step 5**: Learning Zone (The active flashcard interface).

### Learning Zone
The final interface where flashcards are displayed and audio is played. Managed by `#learning-zone` and `display.js`.

### Neural Assets
Assets (Audio/Icons) that are "linked" to data objects rather than hardcoded in HTML, allowing for a dynamic 1000+ asset library.

## 🛡️ SAFETY & SECURITY TERMS

### Parental Gate
A 3-second continuous hold requirement on specific UI elements (like Settings) to prevent accidental child interaction.

### Spam Guard
A 400ms time-lock on interaction buttons to prevent rapid clicking that would cause audio "popping" or logic race conditions.

### Developer Bypass
A keyboard shortcut (`Ctrl+Alt+Shift+D`) that toggles safety locks and security protocols for faster development and testing.

---

## 🎨 UI & INTERACTION TERMS

### Glassmorphism
The visual style of the Navbar and UI panels, characterized by semi-transparency and `-webkit-backdrop-filter: blur(20px)` / `backdrop-filter: blur(20px)`.

### 3D Flip System
The animation system in `cards.css` that allows flashcards to rotate 180 degrees to reveal their "Back" side (Word + Icon).

### Fit-Screen Grid
A dynamic layout mode where card sizes are mathematically calculated to fit the maximum possible area without scrolling.
