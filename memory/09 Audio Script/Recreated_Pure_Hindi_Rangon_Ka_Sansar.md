## Blue Green

### 1. Light Blue (Halka Neela)
**समय**: 25-30 seconds
**फ़ाइल**: `rangon_ka_sansar/lightblue.mp3`
**मॉडल**: en-IN-NeerjaExpressiveNeural
**स्क्रिप्ट**:
"Aasman ki taraf dekhiye bachchon! Kya aapko saaf aur sundar aasman dikh raha hai? Yeh hai halka neela rang! Bolo, Halka Neela! Jaise neela samundar aur khili hui dhoop mein aasman ka rang. Halka neela rang kitna shantipurn lagta hai na? Kya aap apni shirt ya gubbare mein halka neela rang dhoondh sakte ho? Are waah, mil gaya! Bahut badhiya! Chalo ek baar phir milkar zor se bolo, Halka Neela!"
